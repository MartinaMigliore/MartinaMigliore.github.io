Grilled cheese sandwiches with bacon and tomato

Ingredients

  • 8 slices whole wheat or multi-grain bread
  • 4 tablespoons Land O Lakes® Butter, softened
  • 8 (3/4-ounce) slices Land O Lakes® Deli American
  • 8 thin slices tomato, patted dry
  • 8 slices crisply cooked bacon
  • 12 to 16 spinach leaves, if desired

Instruction

Spread 1 side of each bread slice with 1/2 tablespoon butter. Layer 1 slice cheese, 2 slices tomato, 2 slices bacon, 3 to 4 spinach leaves and 1 slice cheese onto each of 4 slices bread, buttered-side down.
Top with remaining bread slices, buttered-side up. Heat 10-inch skillet over medium heat.
Place 1 sandwich into skillet; cook, turning once, 8-10 minutes or until golden brown and cheese is melted. Repeat with remaining sandwiches.

BON APPETIT!
